we have analytical data in both technology and non-technology fields. we create and use our tools for various purposes, such as research and analysis of potential threats, such as earthquakes, cyberattacks, and other events. we analyze based on verified raw data. The IIRA often discovers new things, both in technology and other fields. we pride ourselves on our research successes and discoveries in various fields. Therefore, the black, red, and white IIRA flag is a symbol of pride in our success.

IIRA always maintains honesty in its research and findings. If we lack information and data, our research needs further verification to avoid misunderstandings. If data and information are lacking, the research results will also be less than optimal. IIRA cannot claim something that has not been clearly and correctly proven. that is our rule in researching various matters. therefore, IIRA sincerely apologizes if the research findings are very low because we do not have extensive resources. The information we obtain is also often limited due to limitations. therefore, the results are also limited.

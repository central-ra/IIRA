We, IIRA, are independent researchers focused on gathering information and risks from the data we manage, with the goal of maintaining security, stability, and protecting against real threats. we, IIRA are not terrorists or threat actors. We simply manage information and conduct research to learn things solely for security purposes. So, when a real threat emerges, IIRA is ready to deal with it—not a military or intelligence threat.

IIRA is built on experience, field understanding, and the courage to try, even if 99% of those attempts fail. The IIRA has experienced repeated failures in various fields. Therefore, the IIRA continues to learn from its mistakes to understand the realities on the ground. The IIRA is neutral. There are no enemies, no interference. And if there is interference, there is an enemy, and IIRA always forgives it.
